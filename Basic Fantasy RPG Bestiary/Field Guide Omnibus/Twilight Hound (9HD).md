A Twilight Hound, also known as Orthrus, is a large two-headed canine from another plane of existence brought over to act as guardian of a place or a treasure. It has a serpentine tail that might not be noticed at first. Whenever the twilight hound is mortally wounded, the snake tail will bite the nearest creature viciously; the victim must save vs. Poison or die, after which the hound will dissolve into nothing as it returns to its native plane.

It is said that one of the heads can see invisible objects and beings, while the other head can never be blinded. While in this world, a twilight hound requires no nourishment and never sleeps, as if unaffected by the passage of time.
