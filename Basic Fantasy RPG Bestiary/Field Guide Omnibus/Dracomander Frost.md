A Frost Dracomander looks like a giant lizard with six legs and a pair of dragon wings. Its scales are the colors of ice: white, pale gray, and pale blue. A frost dracomander is very cold, and all non-cold-resistant creatures within 20 feet suffer 1d8 points of damage per round from the cold. It is completely immune to all types of cold-based attacks.
