The least powerful of the true celestials, celestial messengers exist to deliver messages from the gods to mortals, and as such, are the celestials most commonly encountered by in the mortal realm.

Appearing as slender humanoids, marked only by their serene beauty, shimmering silver hair, and the large, feathered wings on their back, Celestial messengers possess a limited form of telepathy, allowing them to communicate with any creature within 50 feet without needing to speak.
