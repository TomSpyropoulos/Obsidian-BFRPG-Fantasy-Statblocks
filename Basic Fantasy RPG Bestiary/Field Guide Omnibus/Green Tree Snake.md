Green tree snakes are arboreal, non-venomous snakes, reaching up to 5 feet in length. Most at home in the trees, their usual prey is rodents, lizards, and birds.

Excellent climbers, these snakes are often be found coiled around tree branches, while their bite is relatively harmless, it may, rarely cause a mild allergic reaction in some individuals.
