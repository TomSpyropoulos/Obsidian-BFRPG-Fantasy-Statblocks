Gas Spores are strange, non-intelligent fungal creatures that strongly resemble some form of oculus (as described on page 195), especially in poorly-lit areas; unless examined closely (within 10 inches) characters are 90% likely to mistake the gas spore for one of the various forms of flying eye monster.
