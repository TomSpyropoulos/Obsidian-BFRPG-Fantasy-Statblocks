Created by a wizard that transformed his pet rabbits into cuddly but fearsome guard animals. The Orycleo are large cat-like beasts. They posses large lion-like bodies, a ferocious appetite, large rabbit ears and a fluffy tuft of fur at the end of their tails.

They live in large underground burrows that can be found deep in the woods, but go above ground to hunt and play.

Orycleo posses Darkvision to a range of 60 feet, and prefer to be left alone. Orycleos don't usually hunt humans, unless hungry or provoked.
