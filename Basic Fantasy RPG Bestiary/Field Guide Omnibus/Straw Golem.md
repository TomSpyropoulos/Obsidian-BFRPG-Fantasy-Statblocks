Certain druids can make a Straw Golem, which typically has an agricultural role and is often used as a scarecrow. A straw golem takes half damage from blunt weapons and no damage from piercing weapons. The golem will usually attack by grabbing a nearby pitchfork, rake, or other discarded tool.
