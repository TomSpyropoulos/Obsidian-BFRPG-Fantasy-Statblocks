An Eelbat looks like an eyeless eel with bat-like wings and gray slimy skin. Despite the lack of visual organs, it can sense its surroundings like a bat and it effectively has Darkvision out to 60 feet. It attacks by biting, and after a successful bite it will hang on and drain an additional 1d6 points of damage every round.
