An Ettercap's appearance is a cross between a grossly-bloated spider and a humanoid. It is often found in the company of 2-4 large spiders. An ettercap averages 6 feet tall and weighs about 200 pounds, and speaks Common.

An ettercap is not a brave creature, but its cunning traps often ensure that the enemy never draws a weapon. When an ettercap does engage its enemies, it attacks with its keen-edged claws and venomous bite. It usually will not come within melee reach of any foe that is still able to move.
