Giant Flying Foxes are a special variety of giant bats. It is similar in appearance to some jungle fruit bats, only much larger and carnivorous. A giant flying fox has typical bat senses, having a natural sonar that grants it Darkvision to a range of 90 feet.

A giant flying fox has a wingspan over 15 feet and weighs over 200 pounds.
