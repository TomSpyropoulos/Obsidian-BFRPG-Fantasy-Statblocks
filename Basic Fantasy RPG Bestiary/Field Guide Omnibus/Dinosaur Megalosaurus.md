A Megalosaurus is a member of the theropod family of bipedal carnivorous dinosaurs, which is best known for the tyrannosaurus rex. Adults are about 30 feet in length, with large heads and toothy jaws. They are apex predators found in forest and savanna habitats, preying on stegosaurs and various sauropods.
