The Wardens are the commanders of both the celestial solars and the celestial guardians. These very tall humanoid figures appear in unique and elaborate armor, complete with full helmets that conceal their features. They have large white swans wings that allow them to fly, and can teleport at-will and without failure.

In battle, the wardens are formidable opponents. They wield massive enchanted two-handed weapons, with which their attacks deal an additional 3 points of damage. They have the ability to teleport and strike in the same round, and can also cast spells as a 16th-level Cleric, and can communicate telepathically within one mile with any being, even though they never vocalise.
