The undead Grave Sentinels and their leaders keep quiet watch over ancient places of death, usually battlefields or tombs. They appear as warriors in smooth black plate armor inlaid with subtle lines of gold or silver. They wear helmets with solid faceplates with no hole for eyes. They use the common arms of the fighting trade but most often bear swords and shields.
