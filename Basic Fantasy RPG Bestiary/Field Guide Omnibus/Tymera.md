Tymeras are a colder climate variant of the fierce three-headed chimera. Where the chimera has lion portions, the tymera consists of black- and white-striped arctic tiger parts. The goat head is more akin to a mountain ram, and the dragon portion is that of an ice dragon. A tymera weighs substantially more than its chimera counterpart at about 5,000 pounds.

A tymera is intelligent but exceptionally ill-tempered. It can speak Dragon and may form pacts with other powerful creatures.
