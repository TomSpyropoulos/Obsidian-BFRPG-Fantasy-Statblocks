An Octopus Fungi appears similar to other giant fungi such as wailing morels (shriekers). If anyone tries to pass an octopus fungi, it will "unfurl" into a single long tentacle and try to wrap around the victim and drag them back to the biting core. A normal attack roll is made; a hit deals 1d6 points of damage from the clawed grasping sucker and a victim must save vs. Dragon Breath, or be drug back to the core the next round. If pulled into the body, two attacks will be made (the tentacle and the mouth) with a +4 bonus each.
