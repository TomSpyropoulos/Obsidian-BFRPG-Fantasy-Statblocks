A Gorgosaurus is a member of the theropod family of bipedal carnivorous dinosaurs, which is best known for the tyrannosaurus rex, and in fact gorgosaurus is believed to be a close relative to the tyrannosaurus. They are apex predators found in floodplain habitats, preying upon hadrosaurid dinosaurs like edmontosaurus or iguanadon.
