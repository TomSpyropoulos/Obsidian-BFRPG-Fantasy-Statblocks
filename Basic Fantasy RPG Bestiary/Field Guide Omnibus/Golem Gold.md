Gold Golems were originally made by a pharaoh as an ostentatious display of wealth. Although it is powerful in combat, its cost far exceeds its utility, therefore it is quite rare to encounter one. It is always womanly-shaped, wearing the semblance of costly robes (which are part of the golem, and thus made of animated gold). It fights with large golden crooks.

A gold golem contains an amount of gold sufficient to mint 80,000 gp.
