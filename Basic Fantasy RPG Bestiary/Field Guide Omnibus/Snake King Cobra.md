King Cobras are deadly and aggressive snakes, They use their spreading hood to warn other creatures not to bother them, while making a unique, among snakes growling noise rather than a hiss. They can grow up to 18 feet in length, and are found throughout the worlds jungles. Primarily feeding on other snakes, they will still attack jumanoids when they feel threatened.
