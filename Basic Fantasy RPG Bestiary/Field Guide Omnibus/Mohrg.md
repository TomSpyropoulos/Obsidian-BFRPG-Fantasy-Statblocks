A Mohrg is the animated corpse of a mass murderer or other heinous villain. The average mohrg is 5-6 feet tall and weighs about 120 pounds, and can easily be confused with a zombie. A mohrg attacks by striking enemies with its fists, but it may also lash out with its 4-foot long tongue. Whomever the tongue touches must save vs. Paralysis or become paralyzed for 6d4 rounds. Unlike zombies, mohrgs move at full speed and have no penalty to initiative.

As with all undead, they may be Turned by a Cleric (as a vampire), and are immune to sleep, charm, or hold magic. Creatures killed by a mohrg rise after 1d4 days as ordinary zombies under the mohrg's control.
