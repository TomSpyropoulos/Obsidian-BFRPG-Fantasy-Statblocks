A Bone Chimera is an abominable undead monster composed of the skeletons of several different species. It has two heads, five legs (all of different lengths), and a pair of tails. In spite of this odd arrangement it is an effective combatant, able to swiftly move about the battlefield and fight fiercely. One can attack opponents in front with its bites and claws, while assaulting opponents behind with its tails; however, it cannot attack a single opponent with its bite, claws, and tails.
