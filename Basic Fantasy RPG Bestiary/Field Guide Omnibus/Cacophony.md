A Cacophony consists of a band of magically-animated instruments brought about by the lingering attachments of failed bards. It will attempt to find audiences to perform to, and upon finding one it will "perform" (IE. attacks) until either the cacophony or the audience is defeated.

Instruments worth 2d12×100 gp can be obtained if the cacophony is defeated in a non-destructive manner.
