The Velociraptor is a small feathered dinosaur, weighing about 30 pounds and similar in size to a turkey. The velociraptor is an avid predator and a skilled pack-hunter that attacks by biting or leaping and using its formidable claws. Against large prey a velociraptor will bite and hold on in order to use its claws for automatic damage each round; however, while holding its AC drops to 11.
