Despite the name, Jackalwere are not ordinary lycanthropes; their condition is not contagious, for example. These strange creatures can assume the form of a somewhat larger-than-normal jackal or that of a man. They are rarely (1 on 1d6) found leading a pack of ordinary jackals, but otherwise will be hunting by themselves.
